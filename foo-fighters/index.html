Foo Fighters' 'Greatest Hits' Is the Band's Sixth Million-Selling Album | SPIN

Foo Fighters are no stranger to selling millions of albums. According to Billboard, the post-grunge heroes' 1997 effort, The Colour and the Shape, has sold 1.9 million copies, while In Your Honor (2005) has sold 1.5 million, One By One (2002) sold 1.4 million, There Is Nothing Left To Lose (1999) sold 1.3 million, and their 1995 self-titled debut has sold 1.3 million copies.
